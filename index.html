Hi, I'm Colby. A first-generation college graduate from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, my journey has been fueled by a restless curiosity. My academic studies, blending Biology with Data Science, trained me to use a hypothesis-driven, analytical approach to find answers in complex, messy data.

I've found that analytics, machine learning, and new generative AI tools are the perfect way to itch my passion for continuous learning. I love digging into data to build solutions, uncover the "why" behind outcomes, and help people understand data through clear insights.

These projects are a showcase of that mindset. Welcome to my portfolio. I invite you to explore my work.
